WildlifeSystems: configuring apt

Configuring apt

Ubuntu

wget -O /usr/share/keyrings/ws.gpg https://wildlife.systems/ws.gpg echo "Types: deb URIs: https://apt.wildlife.systems Suites: stable Components: main Architectures: all Signed-By: /usr/share/keyrings/ws.gpg" | sudo tee /etc/apt/sources.list.d/ws.sources > /dev/null
